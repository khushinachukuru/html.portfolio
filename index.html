Projects

Penetration Testing Using Metasploit

The primary objective of this case study is to demonstrate the effectiveness of utilizing a combination of Nmap and Metasploit in conducting penetration testing to enhance network security. The methodology involves conducting Nmap scans to identify open ports and services running on target systems. Subsequently, the discovered IP addresses are prioritized based on the severity of identified vulnerabilities and potential impact on network security, Metasploit is then employed to execute exploit modules targeting the identified vulnerabilities, emulating real-world attack scenarios.
